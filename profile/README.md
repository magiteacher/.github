# MagiTeacher 🧠
**AI Resource Generator for Teachers in England**

MagiTeacher is a precision AI engine built exclusively for the English National Curriculum — 
from EYFS and Key Stage 1 through to A-Level (KS5). It generates 28 types of 
professional-grade pedagogical materials in seconds, fully calibrated by year group, 
subject, difficulty, and context.

---

## Why Use an AI Resource Generator?

Teachers in England spend an average of 80% of their preparation time on planning, 
resource creation, and administrative tasks — time that could be spent on teaching. 
MagiTeacher automates that process without sacrificing quality or curriculum compliance.

- ⚡ Generate classroom-ready resources in under 30 seconds
- 🎯 Tailored to your exact class, topic, and difficulty level
- ✏️ Fully editable — paste directly into Word, Google Docs, or your VLE
- 📋 Structured to support Ofsted inspection criteria and demonstrate progression

## How It Works

MagiTeacher transforms 3 inputs into a classroom-ready resource in seconds:

**1. The Class** — Select your Year Group and Subject  
**2. The Resource** — Choose one of 28 resource types and enter your topic  
**3. The Personalisation** — Set difficulty, length, and add context for differentiation  

→ Your resource is generated and ready to copy, edit, and teach.
